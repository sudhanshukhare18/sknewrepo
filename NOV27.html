Social Welfare Society

The purpose of economic development is to improve the social and material well-being of all individuals and social institutions with the goal of achieving the highest possible level of human development. Socioeconomic development, therefore, requires the integration of economic and social development.

In India, we often forget about rural India, even though 68.84per cent of India is compromised of villages. The challenges faced by people in rural and underprivileged area in India are very different from the challenges faced by the people living in the cities.

This is a program that seeks to improve the lives of Children , Adolescents, young people by meeting their physical, mental ,social and health needs and by helping them to build the ability or competencies needed to become successful adults.
